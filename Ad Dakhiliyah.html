Ad Dakhiliya

The heart of the Sultanate

Ad Dakhiliyah Region consist of eight wilayats: Nizwa, Samail, Bahla, Adam, Al Hamra, Manah, Izki and Bid bid.Moreover, its play a major role in the history of Oman , especially in the spread of Islam .

Al Hoota Cave Located in the southern part of the Green Mountain Series. The reason of the name of the village of Hoota, which is located within the cave. It is extends for 5 kilometers under the ground.

Bait Al Safah It’s a cultural landmark located in Al Hamra. It is not just a museum, but its a place that display the authentic Omani life in details through many Omani events and crafts.

Harat Al Aqr It is one of the oldest villages in Nizwa. The Al-Aqar is located beside Nizwa fort. Also, Harat Al Aqr was a place for studying different subjects for students from around the world.

Misfat Al Abriyyeen The Mountainous Village -Misfat Al Abriyeen , Al Hamra , is located in Ad Dakhiliyah region , north-east of Oman . Abriyeen name has been derived from the Tribe name , Al Abri , who live there.

Nizwa Souq is one of the famous and oldest markets in Oman it has a lot of small shops with many diverse and varied goods between expensive and cheap and characterized by its wonderful architectural design.
